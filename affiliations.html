JapanLab is a project designed to re-imagine Japanese Studies for the 21st century. It is based at the University of Texas at Austin but the template is available for use at universities across the world. The program aims to generate a steady stream of Japan-focused educational video games and other Digital Humanities content designed by undergraduate students via semester-long internships.
